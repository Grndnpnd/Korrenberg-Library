Game Master Expansion Guide

The Library of Korranberg is designed to grow with your campaign. Add new volumes by editing the LIBRARY.books array in this file.

How to Add a New Volume

Copy the template below and paste it anywhere in the LIBRARY.books array (before the final closing bracket). Each volume must end with a comma except the last one.

{
  title: "Your Title",
  author: "Author Name",
  category: "history",
  year: "998 YK",
  restricted: false,
  content: () => `
    <p>Your content here.</p>
    <h3>Section Headers</h3>
    <p class="marginalia">Sidebar notes use this class.</p>
  `
},

Available Categories (Colleges)

  • history — College of Chronicles
  • arcana — College of the Arcane
  • bestiary — College of Living Things
  • geography — College of the Cartographers
  • cosmology — College of the Outer Spheres
  • prophecy — College of the Prophecy
  • houses — College of the Marks
  • philosophy — College of Thought

The Codex Vault (Restricted)

Set restricted: true to seal a volume. It will not appear in the Stacks or Atrium until the Vault passphrase is spoken.

Formatting

Content supports HTML: <p>, <h3> (copper headers), <h4> (sub-headers), <strong>, <em>, and <p class="marginalia"> for archivist notes.
